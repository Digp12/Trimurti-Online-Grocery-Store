Our Journey

Established in 2001 by Mr. Sanjay Pandurang Patil, Trimurti Kirana and General Stores began with the goal of serving the daily needs of individuals and local customers. Our first store was set up to offer a wide range of quality groceries and essentials in a friendly, accessible environment.

In 2006, we expanded our reach by opening a new outlet in Ozarde. Responding to the growing demand, we evolved again in 2012 to begin offering wholesale services to businesses in need of bulk grocery supplies.

Our Presence

Our main store is located in Islampur, while our larger and strategically placed warehouse is based in Ozarde, enabling efficient service for both retail and wholesale customers.

Our Vision & Digital Transformation

With more than two decades of experience, we understand the evolving needs of customers. To keep up with modern shopping trends and improve convenience, we are proud to introduce our new online platform. This digital initiative is our step forward in offering a seamless, efficient, and hassle-free grocery procurement experience for all businesses.
